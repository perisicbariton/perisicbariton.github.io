Milan Perišić

Discover

Current & Recent Engagements (2026)

Milan Perišić has started the 2026 season with high-level international visibility. In January, he returned to the Gran Teatre del Liceu as the Steuermann in a new production of Tristan und Isolde. That same month, he was a Finalist at the 63rd Tenor Viñas Competition in Barcelona.

In February, he made his house debut at ABAO Bilbao Opera as Cecil in Donizetti's Maria Stuarda. Later in the year, he returns to the Liceu for La bohème and will appear as the Heerrufer in Lohengrin at Opera Tenerife.

2024/2025 Season Highlights

In 2025, his portrayal of Conte d'Almaviva in Le nozze di Figaro with Òpera Catalunya was hailed as "splendid" and "impeccable" by Scherzo. His Puccini interpretations have garnered equal praise; performing Lescaut in Manon Lescaut and Marcello in La bohème (Olyrix).

Key engagements included his debut at the Opéra Bastille as the Député flamand in Don Carlo and returning to the Liceu as Bernardo in West Side Story.

Past Seasons & Early Highlights

In 2023, he performed in Shostakovich's The Nose at Teatro Real and Antony & Cleopatra at the Liceu. In 2022, he sang the title role in Don Giovanni in Spoleto and Perugia.
