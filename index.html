About Milan

Born in Novi Sad, Serbia, his music studies span across various fields: Accordion, Music theory, Composition (Bachelor's degree at the Academy of Arts in his hometown) and Voice (Master's degree at the Tartini Conservatory in Trieste).

In 2023 he won three consecutive first prizes at international competitions: Les Corts (Barcelona), Josep Palet (Martorell) and BA Lirica (Busto Arsizio). In 2025 he won the Festival de Música de Mafra "Filipe de Sousa" special prize at the 2nd Cascais Opera International Competition, and was a finalist at the 63rd Tenor Viñas Competition among a record-breaking 712 competitors.

Repertoire & Future Engagements

In his repertoire are roles such as Il Conte d'Almaviva (Le nozze di Figaro), Dottore Malatesta (Don Pasquale), Lescaut (Manon Lescaut), Marcello (La bohème), and Don Giovanni.

Among his next role debuts we find Schaunard (La Bohème), Cecil (Maria Stuarda), and Heerrufer (Lohengrin).
